Thousand Year Old Vampire

Build your vampire in rules order: mortal life, mortals, skills/resources, additional memories, then the curse.

Step 1

1/5
  1. Identity + first Memory
  2. At least 3 Mortals
  3. 3 Skills + 3 Resources
  4. Three more Memories
  5. Immortal + Mark + final Memory

Start with who they were before undeath, then record the first Memory.

    Create at least three mortal Characters tied to your past.

      Add three Skills and three Resources from mortal life.

          Add three more Memories (for a total of four before the curse).
