Pre-Prompt 1

Thousand Year Old Vampire

Build the mortal life, the curse, and the five starting memories before the prompt deck begins.

Identity

Name the vampire and use the first memory to summarize their mortal life.

Memories

Add five starting memories. Each begins with one Experience; the fifth should cover the curse and transformation.

    Characters

    Start with at least three mortals and one immortal who brought the curse.

      Skills

      Add the three capabilities your character possessed before undeath.

        Resources

        Add three mortal-world assets, from keepsakes to estates or warships.

          Marks

          Record the visible sign of undeath that follows the vampire forever.
